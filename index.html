Welcome to maptual:kabul

maptual:kabul is a city map of Kabul, Afghanistan offering the most accurate, exclusive dataset in existence for the Afghan capital city.

Once downloaded, the map is available fully offline within the app, eliminating the need for an expensive, unreliable data connection for map viewing on-the-move. While in Kabul, you can even see your location via GPS without any data connection.

When you are connected to the Internet, you can toggle between map layers, comparing Google MapsTM Satellite, Standard or Hybrid views to get a better picture of where you are, or try the community-updated OpenStreetMap layer for further context around your location.

Map data includes:

  • √ Roads and highways, including field-verified street names
  • √ Rivers, streams, lakes, and ponds
  • √ Special land use, like green areas
  • √ Commercial, residential, and industrial zones
  • √ Neighborhood and district names
  • √ Historical places
  • √ Buildings

These comparative visuals offer variety of ways to interpret city assets for the most complete representation of the Afghan capital available today.
